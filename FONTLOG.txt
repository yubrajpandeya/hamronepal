Release Version: 1.0  
Release Date: July 2025

--------------------------------------------------
1. Overview
--------------------------------------------------

Hamro Nepal is a modern Devanagari typeface designed specifically for the Nepali language. It blends traditional aesthetics of the script with modern digital clarity and usability. It supports the complete Devanagari Unicode block, including all standard Nepali vowels, consonants, matras, punctuation, and basic ligatures.

The font is suitable for:
- UI design (mobile and web apps)
- Branding and logo design
- Print publications (newspapers, books, flyers)
- Educational and government documents
